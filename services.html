Our Services

A complete 360-degree real estate service for property owners who are not always in the country.

Core Services

What We Do

From management to development — every service backed by compliance, transparency, and international standards.

🏢

1. Professional Property Management

For owners who want passive income without the headaches.

  • Tenant Sourcing: Marketing, background checks, credit verification, and lease negotiation
  • Rent Collection: On-time collection, deposit management, and monthly remittances
  • Maintenance & Repairs: Scheduled upkeep, emergency repairs, and 24/7 hotline for tenants
  • Financial Reporting: Monthly income statements and annual summaries sent directly to you
  • Legal Compliance: Proper lease contracts, eviction handling, and LGU/BIR compliance
  • Dual Mode: AI-Assisted (predictive pricing, real-time dashboards) or Legacy (human-verified, paper-trail)
Who this is for: OFWs with a vacant condo in BGC, Makati CBD, Tagaytay City or Boracay; expats who moved back home but kept their house in Baguio City; retirees who spend half the year abroad.
📈

2. Strategic Property Disposition (Selling)

For owners ready to cash out or reallocate capital.

  • Licensed Brokers: PRC-accredited Real Estate Brokers handling all transactions
  • Full Compliance: Real Estate Service Act (RA 9646) adherence on every sale
  • Market Valuation: Based on actual comparable sales, not inflated hopes
  • Global Marketing: Reach through local and international channels
  • Legal Soundness: From Letter of Intent to Deed of Absolute Sale
  • AMLA Compliance: Buyer due diligence, source of funds verification, and CTR/STR filing
Who this is for: Inherited property you don't want to manage, downsizing after retirement, or converting illiquid land into cash for other investments.
🏗️

3. Development & Value-Add Projects

For landowners with underutilized property. We don't just sell your land as-is. We develop it to unlock its full potential.

  • Residential: Subdivisions, townhouses, mid-rise condominiums, 4PH government housing
  • Eco-Tourism: Resorts, wellness communities, coastal forest retreats
  • Industrial: PEZA-registered ecozones, logistics parks, industrial estates
  • Mixed-Use: Residential + commercial + retail within one master plan
  • Smart Cities: Sustainable urban communities with integrated technology
  • Agricultural Conversion: Transforming idle farmland into productive agro-industrial or eco-tourism assets
Who this is for: Family-owned agricultural land sitting idle, large lots needing subdivision approval, or properties in tourism zones waiting for the right developer.
🤝

4. Joint Ventures & Strategic Alliances

For landowners who want to share risk and reward.

  • Structure Options: Contractual JV, Corporate JV (new SPC), or Co-Development Agreement
  • Land-for-Equity: You contribute the land; we contribute development, construction, marketing, and sales
  • Profit Sharing: Aligned with capital contributions and risk allocation
  • Transparent Governance: Board oversight, quarterly reporting, and independent audit rights
  • Full Compliance: PCAB, DHSUD, and SEC rules strictly observed
  • Exit Strategies: Pre-agreed buyout options and dissolution protocols
Who this is for: Landowners who own land but lack capital or development expertise to unlock its full value.

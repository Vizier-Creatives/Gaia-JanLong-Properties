About Gaia-Jan Long Properties

Gaia-Jan Lng Properties was born from a vision: to make self‑sustainable, nature‑integrated living accessible to everyone. Based in Sumbawa, Indonesia, we work directly with local communities and conservation experts to offer land and homes that respect the environment.

Our mission is to connect conscious buyers with exotic, off‑grid properties that are ready for permaculture, renewable energy, and regenerative lifestyles.

Every property we list undergoes an ecological assessment, and we provide ongoing support to help you build your dream eco‑village.

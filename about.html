About Gaia-Jan Long

Stewards of land. Builders of legacy. Bridging international standards with Philippine expertise.

Our Story

We Steward Your Investment As If It Were Our Own

Gaia-Jan Long Properties Inc. is the dedicated and exclusive real estate property management, development, and disposition arm of two distinguished groups: the Golden Dragon Group (Philippines) and the ClaMor Leisure Group.

We are structured as a Special Purpose Company (SPC) — a legal entity formed to focus entirely on one mission: managing, developing, and maximizing the value of real estate assets for property owners who cannot always be physically present.

Whether you are an Overseas Filipino Worker (OFW), a dual citizen, or a foreign expatriate who owns property in the Philippines, we become your eyes, ears, and hands on the ground.

Our Articles of Incorporation expressly state no geographical limitation. We conduct business anywhere in the world, subject only to local laws and Philippine regulations.

Etymology

The Meaning Behind Our Name

Every element of our name carries deep significance — from ancient Greek wisdom to Chinese philosophical tradition.

Gaia
Greek for "Mother Earth"

Representing our commitment to sustainable, eco-conscious development and responsible land stewardship. Just as Gaia nourishes all life, we nurture every property under our care — ensuring it thrives for generations.

金 (Jīn)
Jan — "Golden"

金 represents radiant gold, symbolizing profound grace (优雅, yōu yǎ) and fresh new beginnings (新生, xīn shēng). Just as gold endures through refinement, we pledge to gracefully transform underutilized properties — dormant lands or structures — into thriving, income-generating assets that herald prosperity and renewal for communities and investors alike.

龙 (Lóng)
Long — "Dragon"

龙 is the majestic Chinese dragon, embodying long-term vision (长远眼光, cháng yuǎn yǎn guāng) and eternal strength. We shun fleeting "quick flips" for enduring legacies, crafting developments that deliver lasting value — resilient infrastructure, sustained returns, and generational wealth in real estate and beyond.

Properties Inc.
We Are Not Agents

We are owners, managers, and developers of real assets. Our corporate structure as an incorporated entity with ₱100 million authorized capital stock reflects our institutional commitment to accountability, transparency, and long-term partnership.

Regulatory Foundation

The Legal Backbone

We are not a fly-by-night operation. Our legal foundation is built for accountability, transparency, and compliance.

SEC

Securities & Exchange Commission

Fully registered stock corporation with ₱100 Million authorized capital stock under the Revised Corporation Code (RA 11232).

AMLC

Anti-Money Laundering Council

Registered as a "Covered Person" under RA 9160. We verify source of funds and report large cash transactions.

DHSUD

Department of Human Settlements

Licensed to sell real estate projects to the public. License to Sell required before any offering.

PCAB

Philippine Contractors Accreditation

We build our own projects without a contractor's license (permitted by law). For third-party construction, we secure proper PCAB licenses.

RESA

Real Estate Service Act

All brokerage activities supervised by a licensed Real Estate Broker (PRC-accredited) per RA 9646.

DATA PRIVACY

National Privacy Commission

Your personal and financial information is protected under the Data Privacy Act (RA 10173).
